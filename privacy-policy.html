Collecting and Using Your Data

Types of Data Collected

The App does not collect Personal Data. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location, or device identifiers.

The only data stored by the App is an anonymous list of words a user voluntarily saves ("saved words"). This data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore) with no user account, device identifier, or any personal information linked to it. Saved words are used solely to allow users to revisit words they have chosen to keep, and can be cleared at any time within the App.

Use of Your Data

The anonymous saved word data is used only to provide the save/recall feature within the App. It is not used for analytics, advertising, profiling, or any other purpose.

Retention of Your Data

Anonymous saved word data is retained in Firestore for as long as the App is in use. Because this data contains no identifying information, We have no way to link it to a specific user or device. Users may clear their saved words at any time from within the App.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following Google (Google LLC) services to operate:

  • Google Firebase Cloud Functions — serves the daily Chinese word to the App. No user or device data is included in these requests.
  • Google Firebase Firestore — stores the user's anonymous saved word list. No personal information is associated with this data.
  • Google Translate Text-to-Speech — generates audio pronunciation of Chinese words. Only the Chinese character string is transmitted; no user data is sent.

Google's privacy policy governs data processed through these services: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

The App does not include analytics services, advertising networks, or any other third-party SDKs beyond those listed above.

Sharing of Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or share user data with any third parties except the Google Firebase infrastructure described above, which is required to operate the Service. We do not share data with advertisers, data brokers, or any other parties.

We may disclose data if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g. a court or government agency).

Security

The security of Your data is important to Us. Firestore security rules ensure that only the App's backend functions can write word content; direct writes from clients are blocked. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure and We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Links to Other Websites

Our Service does not contain links to third-party websites. The third-party services listed above (Google) are used solely to operate the App and are not browsable by users.
